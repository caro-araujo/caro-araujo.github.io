About

Hi! My name is Maria Elena.

I’m an airbnb host in Loja, Ecuador since 2016. I love traveling, meeting new people and showing them around the city. The available properties are inside a complex where I also live with my family, my husband Luis, my two daughters Carolina and Doménica, as well as my son Luis and our german shepherd Lukas. I have created this blog to tell a bit more about our family and also to share all the adventures that our guests have with us.
